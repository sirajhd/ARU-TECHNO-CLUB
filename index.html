About Us

Welcome to the ARU Techno Club! We are a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts at Arsi University, dedicated to exploring the latest advancements in technology and fostering innovation among students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to collaborate, learn, and share their knowledge in various fields of technology, including programming, robotics, artificial intelligence, and more.

Our Activities

We organize workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures to inspire creativity and hands-on experience. Join us as we embark on exciting projects, develop new skills, and connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for technology.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced techie, there's a place for you in the ARU Techno Club! Together, let's innovate and make a difference in the tech world.
